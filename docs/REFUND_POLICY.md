# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2026  
**VwaTek Apply - Job Search Assistant**

## Overview

VwaTek Apply ("we", "us", "our") is committed to customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines our commitment to fair practices and your rights as a customer, in compliance with Canadian consumer protection laws including the Consumer Protection Act (Ontario) and applicable provincial regulations.

## 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a **30-day money-back guarantee** on all subscription purchases. If you are not satisfied with your subscription for any reason, you may request a full refund within 30 days of your initial purchase date.

### Eligibility for 30-Day Guarantee

- **New subscribers only**: The 30-day guarantee applies to your first purchase of a paid subscription (Pro or Premium)
- **One-time use**: This guarantee can only be used once per customer account
- **Full refund**: You will receive a complete refund of the subscription amount paid

## General Refund Policy

### Annual Subscriptions

For annual subscriptions purchased beyond the 30-day guarantee period:

- **Pro-rated refunds**: You may receive a pro-rated refund for the unused portion of your subscription if you cancel within the first 6 months
- **No refund after 6 months**: Refunds are not available for annual subscriptions cancelled after 6 months of the billing period
- **Calculation method**: Pro-rated refunds are calculated based on full months remaining in your subscription term

### Monthly Subscriptions

For monthly subscriptions:

- **Current billing period**: No refunds for the current billing period once payment has been processed
- **Cancel anytime**: You may cancel at any time to prevent future charges
- **Service continues**: Your subscription features remain active until the end of your current billing period

## Automatic Renewal Disclosure

In accordance with Canadian law, we provide the following disclosures about automatic subscription renewals:

### Before Purchase

Prior to your first subscription payment, we clearly disclose:
1. The subscription will automatically renew at the end of each billing period
2. The renewal price (which will be the same as your initial price unless we notify you of changes)
3. The renewal frequency (monthly or yearly, as selected)
4. How to cancel your subscription

### Renewal Notices

- **Annual subscriptions**: We will send you a reminder email at least 30 days before your annual renewal date
- **Monthly subscriptions**: You will receive a billing receipt after each monthly renewal
- **Price changes**: If subscription prices change, we will notify you at least 30 days before your next renewal

### Cancellation Rights

You have the right to cancel your subscription at any time:
- Through your account settings
- By contacting our support team at support@vwatek.com
- Through the Stripe billing portal (for web subscriptions)
- Through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android) for in-app purchases

## How to Request a Refund

### Method 1: Online Request

1. Log into your VwaTek Apply account
2. Navigate to Settings > Subscription > Billing
3. Click "Request Refund"
4. Complete the refund request form
5. Submit your request

### Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to **support@vwatek.com** with:
- Subject line: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"
- Your full name
- Account email address
- Date of purchase
- Subscription plan (Pro or Premium)
- Reason for refund request (optional but helpful)

### Method 3: Contact Support

Contact our customer support team:
- Email: support@vwatek.com
- Response time: Within 2 business days

## Refund Processing

### Processing Time

- **Review period**: We will review your refund request within 2 business days
- **Approval notification**: You will receive an email confirming the refund decision
- **Payment processing**: Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days
- **Bank processing**: Depending on your payment method and financial institution, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account

### Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase:
- **Credit/Debit cards**: Refunded to the original card
- **Apple App Store**: Processed through Apple's refund system
- **Google Play Store**: Processed through Google's refund system

## In-App Purchases (iOS and Android)

For subscriptions purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store:

### Apple App Store Purchases

- Refund requests for iOS app purchases must be submitted through Apple
- Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund from Apple
- Apple's refund policies apply in addition to ours

### Google Play Store Purchases

- Refund requests for Android app purchases can be submitted through Google Play
- Visit play.google.com/store/account to manage subscriptions
- Google's refund policies apply in addition to ours

## Exceptions

Refunds may not be available in the following circumstances:

1. **Account violations**: If your account was terminated due to violation of our Terms of Service
2. **Fraudulent activity**: If there is evidence of fraudulent use or abuse
3. **Chargebacks**: If you have already initiated a chargeback with your payment provider
4. **Repeated refunds**: If you have a history of requesting multiple refunds (abuse prevention)

## Consumer Protection Rights

### Your Rights Under Canadian Law

This policy does not limit any rights you may have under applicable Canadian consumer protection legislation, including but not limited to:

- **Ontario**: Consumer Protection Act, 2002
- **Quebec**: Consumer Protection Act

### Cooling-Off Period

Where applicable provincial laws provide for a cooling-off period that exceeds our standard 30-day guarantee, the longer period will apply.

### Direct Contracts

For subscriptions purchased directly through our website (not via App Stores), you have the right to cancel within the cooling-off period specified by your province's consumer protection laws.
