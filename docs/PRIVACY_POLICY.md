## 3. Subscription and Payment Information

### 3.1 Payment Processing

**What We Store:**
- Last four digits of your payment card
- Card type (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)
- Billing address (for tax calculation)
- Subscription plan and status
- Payment history and receipts

**What We Do NOT Store:**
- Full credit card numbers
- CVV/security codes
- Bank account numbers (unless you provide them)

### 3.2 Third-Party Payment Processors

We use secure, PCI-compliant payment processors:

| Platform | Payment Processor | Their Privacy Policy |
|----------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Web | Stripe | https://stripe.com/privacy |
| iOS | Apple | https://apple.com/privacy |
| Android | Google | https://policies.google.com/privacy |

**Stripe Data Handling:**
- Stripe stores your full payment information securely
- We receive only a customer ID and last four digits
- Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified

**App Store Data Handling:**
- Apple/Google process in-app purchases
- We receive subscription status but not payment details
- Refund requests for app store purchases are handled by Apple/Google

### 3.3 Subscription Data We Collect

- Subscription tier (Free, Pro, Premium)
- Billing period (monthly, annual)
- Subscription start and renewal dates
- Cancellation date (if applicable)
- Usage metrics for premium features
- Payment history and invoices

### 3.4 How We Use Subscription Data

- Process and manage your subscription
- Enforce feature limits based on tier
- Send renewal and billing notifications
- Process refund requests
- Generate invoices and receipts
- Detect and prevent fraud

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Where We Store Data

Your data is stored on secure servers in:
- **Primary:** United States (Google Cloud Platform)
- **Backup:** Canada (Google Cloud Platform)

### 5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- Regular security audits
- Intrusion detection systems
- DDoS protection

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Employee background checks
- Access controls and logging
- Security awareness training
- Incident response procedures

### 5.3 Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account data | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Resume/cover letters | Until deleted by user or account deletion |
| Payment records | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Usage logs | 2 years |
| Support tickets | 3 years |

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

### 5.4 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information:
1. We will notify you within 72 hours of discovery
2. We will report to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner as required
3. We will provide information about what happened and steps to protect yourself
