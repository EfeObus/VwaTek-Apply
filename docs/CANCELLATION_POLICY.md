# Cancellation Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2026  
**VwaTek Apply - Job Search Assistant**

## Overview

This Cancellation Policy explains how to cancel your VwaTek Apply subscription and what happens after cancellation. We support your right to cancel at any time with no hidden fees or complicated procedures.

## Your Right to Cancel

You may cancel your VwaTek Apply subscription at any time for any reason. There are no:
- Cancellation fees
- Minimum commitment periods (except for the billing period you've already paid)
- Penalties for early cancellation

## How to Cancel

### Web Subscriptions (Stripe)

If you subscribed through our website:

1. **Log in** to your VwaTek Apply account
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Subscription**
3. Click **"Manage Subscription"**
4. Select **"Cancel Subscription"**
5. Confirm your cancellation

**Alternative method:**
- Email billing@vwatek.com with your account email and cancellation request
- We will process your request within 2 business days

### iOS App (Apple App Store)

If you subscribed through the iOS app:

1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone/iPad
2. Tap your **Apple ID** at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Select **VwaTek Apply**
5. Tap **"Cancel Subscription"**
6. Confirm your cancellation

**Alternative method:**
- Open the App Store app
- Tap your profile icon
- Tap **Subscriptions**
- Select VwaTek Apply and cancel

**Note:** Apple manages all iOS subscriptions. We cannot process iOS cancellations directly.

### Android App (Google Play)

If you subscribed through the Android app:

1. Open the **Google Play Store** app
2. Tap your **profile icon**
3. Tap **Payments & subscriptions** > **Subscriptions**
4. Select **VwaTek Apply**
5. Tap **"Cancel subscription"**
6. Follow the on-screen instructions

**Alternative method:**
- Go to play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions
- Find VwaTek Apply and click "Manage"
- Click "Cancel subscription"

**Note:** Google manages all Android subscriptions. We cannot process Android cancellations directly.

## What Happens After Cancellation

### Access Continues Until Period Ends

When you cancel your subscription:

1. **Your cancellation is processed immediately**
2. **No further charges** will be made to your payment method
3. **You keep premium access** until the end of your current billing period
4. **After your billing period ends**, your account automatically converts to Free tier

### Example Timeline

| Action | Date | Result |
|--------|------|--------|
| You subscribe | January 1 | Premium access begins |
| You cancel | January 15 | Cancellation confirmed |
| Account converts | February 1 | Account becomes Free tier |

### Data Retention

After downgrading to Free tier:

- **All your data is preserved** (resumes, cover letters, interview notes)
- You can still **view** all your content
- **Feature access** becomes limited to Free tier limits
- You can **re-subscribe anytime** to regain premium access

### What You Lose on Free Tier

After cancellation takes effect, you will no longer have access to:

**From Pro:**
- Extended resume/cover letter limits
- Extended AI enhancement limits
- Salary insights and benchmarks
- Unlimited application tracking

**From Premium (in addition to Pro features):**
- Negotiation coach
- LinkedIn profile optimizer
- Priority support
- Unlimited everything

## Cooling-Off Period

### Canadian Consumer Protection

Under Canadian consumer protection laws, you have specific cancellation rights:

#### Internet Agreement Cooling-Off (Ontario)

If you are an Ontario resident and meet certain conditions under the Consumer Protection Act, 2002, you may have 7 days to cancel an internet agreement.

#### Quebec Consumer Protection

Quebec residents have additional rights under the Quebec Consumer Protection Act, including the right to cancel distance contracts in certain circumstances.

### Our Extended Policy

Regardless of your province, we offer:

- **30-day money-back guarantee** on first subscription (see [Refund Policy](REFUND_POLICY.md))
- **Immediate processing** of all cancellation requests
- **No questions asked** cancellation policy

## Auto-Renewal Cancellation

### Stopping Auto-Renewal

Canceling your subscription stops automatic renewal. To prevent your next renewal charge:

| Subscription Type | Cancel By |
|-------------------|-----------|
| Monthly | At least 24 hours before your renewal date |
| Annual | At least 24 hours before your renewal date |

### Renewal Reminders

We send the following reminders:
- **Annual subscriptions**: Email 30 days and 7 days before renewal
- **Monthly subscriptions**: Receipt after each successful charge

## Special Circumstances

### Payment Disputes

If you have a billing dispute:
1. Contact us first at billing@vwatek.com
2. We will investigate within 2 business days
3. If you're entitled to a refund, we'll process it within 5-10 business days

**Please avoid filing chargebacks** with your bank before contacting us. Unauthorized chargebacks may result in account suspension.

### Technical Issues

If you're canceling due to technical issues:
1. Contact support@vwatek.com first
2. We may be able to resolve your issue
3. If not resolved, we offer extended refund options

### Medical or Financial Hardship

If you're experiencing hardship:
- Contact support@vwatek.com to discuss options
- We may offer subscription pauses, discounts, or extended refunds
- All hardship cases are reviewed individually and treated confidentially

## Resubscribing

### After Cancellation

You can resubscribe at any time:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
3. Choose a plan and complete payment

### What You Get Back

When you resubscribe:
- All your previous data and content are restored
- Premium features are immediately available
- Your usage limits reset for the new billing period

### Promotional Pricing

- Previous promotional rates are not automatically honored on resubscription
- Contact support@vwatek.com for available promotions
- Annual subscribers may be eligible for loyalty discounts

## Enterprise Cancellations

For Enterprise/Organization accounts:
- Only Account Owners or Admins can cancel
- Contact enterprise@vwatek.com to initiate cancellation
- 30-day notice may be required depending on contract terms
- Data export assistance is available upon request

## Confirmation

### Cancellation Confirmation

After canceling, you will receive:
1. **Immediate on-screen confirmation** with end date
2. **Email confirmation** within 24 hours
3. **Reminder** 3 days before your subscription ends

### No Confirmation Received?

If you don't receive confirmation:
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Verify you canceled through the correct platform (web, iOS, or Android)
3. Contact support@vwatek.com to verify your cancellation status
